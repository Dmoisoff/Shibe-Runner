Shibe Dog Runner

Created by Daniel Moisoff

The inspiration for the theme of this game came from my recent trip to Japan. I loved the culture, the landscape and the people there.

Controls

  • Press the space bar or the up arrow key to jump
  • The longer you hold the key the higher you will jump
  • Press p to pause
